# Reposting Strategy: Personal Profile to Company Page

## Should You Repost Your 7 LinkedIn Posts?

**Short Answer:** Yes, but with a strategic approach! Don't just copy-paste all at once.

---

## Why Repost Makes Sense

✅ **Build page credibility** - Shows the page has history and content
✅ **Reach new audience** - Company page followers may not follow your personal profile
✅ **Establish authority** - Demonstrates expertise and consistency
✅ **SEO benefits** - More content indexed under Agent Sense brand
✅ **Content library** - Creates a foundation for the page

---

## Strategic Reposting Plan

### Option 1: Staggered Reposting (RECOMMENDED)

**Week 1:**
- Day 1: Launch announcement (new post)
- Day 3: Repost your best-performing post (highest engagement)
- Day 5: Repost your second-best post

**Week 2:**
- Day 1: New episode announcement
- Day 3: Repost #3
- Day 5: Repost #4

**Week 3:**
- Day 1: New content
- Day 3: Repost #5
- Day 5: Repost #6

**Week 4:**
- Day 1: New content
- Day 3: Repost #7
- Day 5: New content

**Why this works:**
- Doesn't flood followers' feeds
- Mixes old and new content
- Builds momentum gradually
- Allows time for engagement on each post

---

### Option 2: Curated Selection

**Only repost your top 3-4 posts:**
- Choose posts with highest engagement
- Focus on evergreen content (not time-sensitive)
- Skip posts that reference "just posted" or specific dates
- Prioritize educational/valuable content over announcements

**Why this works:**
- Quality over quantity
- Best foot forward
- Leaves room for fresh content
- Avoids repetitive feel

---

### Option 3: Refresh and Repost

**Rewrite posts with company page voice:**
- Update from "I" to "We"
- Add company branding elements
- Update any outdated information
- Add new insights or updates
- Include current episode count

**Example:**
**Original (Personal):** "I'm excited to share Episode 3 of Agent Sense..."
**Refreshed (Company):** "Episode 3 of Agent Sense explores multi-agent orchestration..."

**Why this works:**
- Feels fresh, not recycled
- Appropriate for company voice
- Opportunity to improve content
- More professional tone

---

## What to Repost vs. What to Skip

### ✅ REPOST THESE:
- Episode announcements (evergreen)
- Educational content about AI agents
- Technical insights and tips
- Guest spotlights
- Key takeaways from episodes
- Industry observations
- "How-to" content

### ❌ SKIP THESE:
- Personal announcements ("I'm launching...")
- Time-sensitive posts ("Just went live today...")
- Posts with low engagement
- Overly personal content
- Duplicate topics (if you posted similar content twice)
- Posts that don't align with company brand

## Reposting Best Practices

### DO:
✅ Space out reposts (2-3 days between)
✅ Mix with new content
✅ Update any outdated information
✅ Add current episode count
✅ Include clear CTAs
✅ Tag yourself and Frank in reposts
✅ Respond to comments promptly
✅ Track which reposts perform best

### DON'T:
❌ Post all 7 at once
❌ Copy-paste without reviewing
❌ Repost time-sensitive content
❌ Ignore comments on reposts
❌ Repost low-quality content
❌ Use "I" voice on company page
❌ Forget to update links

## Recommended Reposting Order

Based on typical engagement patterns, repost in this order:

1. **Episode highlights with key insights** (highest value)
2. **Educational "how-to" content** (evergreen)
3. **Guest spotlights** (builds credibility)
4. **Industry observations** (thought leadership)
5. **Technical deep dives** (for technical audience)
6. **Behind-the-scenes** (humanizes brand)
7. **General announcements** (lowest priority)

## Quick Decision Framework

For each of your 7 posts, ask:

1. **Is it evergreen?** (Not time-sensitive) → Repost
2. **Did it perform well?** (High engagement) → Repost
3. **Is it educational?** (Provides value) → Repost
4. **Can I improve it?** (Add new insights) → Refresh & repost
5. **Is it too personal?** (About you, not podcast) → Skip
6. **Is it redundant?** (Similar to another post) → Skip one
7. **Does it align with brand?** (Professional, technical) → Repost

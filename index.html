About Agent Sense

Agent Sense is a podcast focused on the enterprise implementation of AI agents and multi-agent systems. We explore the shift from conversational AI to agentic AI that can take action, make decisions, and orchestrate complex workflows across business systems.

Our discussions cover Day 2 and Day 3 enterprise AI implementation, including governance, orchestration, context management, security, and the practical challenges of moving from pilot to production.

## Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is key:** Post 3x per week minimum
2. **Quality over quantity:** Better to post less with high value
3. **Engage authentically:** Real conversations, not just broadcasting
4. **Use video:** LinkedIn prioritizes video content
5. **Tag strategically:** Tag guests, collaborators, relevant companies
6. **Time it right:** Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM
7. **Repurpose content:** Turn episode insights into multiple posts
8. **Build community:** Respond to every comment in first 24 hours

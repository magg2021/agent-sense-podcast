==================================

1. Go to github.com and sign in
2. Click "New repository" (green button)
3. Name it: agent-sense-podcast

5. Click "Create repository"

6. Click "uploading an existing file"

7. DRAG AND DROP these files from your computer:
   - index.html
   - agent_sense_cover.png
   - agent_sense_ep02_cover.png

8. Click "Commit changes"

9. For each episode folder:

   - Click "Add file" → "Upload files"

11. Source: Deploy from branch "main"
12. Click Save
13. Wait 1-2 minutes
14. Your site is live at: https://yourusername.github.io/agent-sense-podcast/
